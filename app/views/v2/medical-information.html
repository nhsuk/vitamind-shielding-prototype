Do not use this service if you:

  • are under the age of 18
  • live in a nursing or residential care home - we will send supplements directly to the home where you live

Prescriptions and other supplements you are already taking

Do not use this service if you:

  • are already taking vitamin D or other supplements that may contain vitamin D
  • are already taking vitamin D that your doctor has prescribed or recommended - continue to take the dose they recommended

Medical conditions

You should not use this service if you have any of these conditions:

  • high vitamin D levels
  • kidney stones (now or in the past)
  • too much parathyroid hormone (hyperparathyroidism)
  • cancer (some cancers can lead to high calcium levels)
  • severe kidney disease
  • a rare illness called sarcoidosis

Taking vitamin D safely

Read how to take vitamin D supplements safely on GOV.UK.

Important

You must not use this service:

  • if you are already taking vitamin D or other supplements
  • if you have any of the medical conditions on this page

Discuss this with your doctor the next time you talk to them.

If you think that vitamin D is suitable for you, please continue.
